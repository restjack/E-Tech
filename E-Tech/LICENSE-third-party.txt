Third-party content included in E-Tech
======================================

Everything in E-Tech is public domain (see LICENSE.txt) EXCEPT the two
ported features below, which keep their original licenses.

1. Uranium bacteria on Gleba (gleba-uranium.lua and graphics/icons/
   uranium-bacteria.png, uranium-bacteria-2.png, uranium-bacteria-cultivation.png)
   is ported from the mod "Simple Gleba Uranium" 1.0.2 by cindersash
   (https://mods.factorio.com/mod/simple-gleba-uranium), used under the
   MIT License reproduced below.

2. Teleporter pads (everything under teleporters/ — code and graphics/sounds)
   are adapted from the mod "Teleporters" 2.0.0 by Klonan
   (https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Teleporters,
   https://github.com/Klonan/Teleporters), used under the GNU Lesser
   General Public License v3.0, reproduced below. These files remain
   LGPLv3-licensed; the public-domain grant in LICENSE.txt does not apply
   to them.
   Modifications by E-Tech: Factorio 2.1 API fixes (entity.active ->
   disabled_by_script), prototype/locale names prefixed etech- so both mods
   can coexist, storage key renamed, sprites inlined without the 1.1-era
   hr_version wrapper, rocket-launch easter egg removed, and E-Tech
   feature additions on top (see changelog.txt).

3. Total productivity (productivity/data.lua) is adapted from the mod
   "Total Productivity" 2.0.x by AivanF
   (https://mods.factorio.com/mod/Productivity,
   https://github.com/AivanF/factorio-AivanF-mods), used under the GNU
   Lesser General Public License v3.0 (the repository's license),
   reproduced below. That file remains LGPLv3-licensed; the public-domain
   grant in LICENSE.txt does not apply to it.
   Modifications by E-Tech: the 28 per-category startup settings condensed
   into 4 group toggles, nil-safe data.raw category lookups, per-recipe
   pcall/error wrapper dropped, settings renamed etech-prod-*.

4. Jetpack fuel HUD (jetpack-ui.lua) is adapted from the mod
   "Puppy's Jetpack UI" 0.2.1 by Puppy
   (https://mods.factorio.com/mod/puppy-jetpack-ui), used under the MIT
   License (Copyright (c) 2022 Puppy) — same license text as reproduced
   below for Simple Gleba Uranium.
   Modifications by E-Tech: flib dependency removed (plain gui
   construction), element names prefixed etech-, and the window-position
   reset bug fixed (saved position is always restored and clamped on the
   display-resolution/scale events instead of being discarded against a
   possibly-stale display_resolution at window creation).
